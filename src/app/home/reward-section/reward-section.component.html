It’s a Human Thing!

Sharing is an innate human mechanism engrained within our DNA. It is tribal; it is instinctive; and it is part of what makes us who we are. People have been sharing since the dawn of time; trading and bartering their goods and services to survive.

Modern Technology allows us to share beyond the geographical barriers and reach out to others to acquire what we need, when and where we need it.

Why sharing feels good:

  1. It is great for the environment. There are trillions of dollars’ worth of unused resources in the world and that's why getting involved in the share economy enables these resources to be utilised more efficiently and saves energy and reduces waste..
  2. You get to connect with your community and meet like-minded people
  3. You become part of a global share community and network of awesome individuals, saving our planet one space at a time.
  4. You get to be as creative as you want, when repurposing your underutilised space i.e. a repurposed garage can be transformed into a gorgeous Yoga Studio...
  5. By sharing unique spaces for others to enjoy at a fair price; you can earn spare cash – so everyone WINS!
  6. Share your unique skills and knowledge in the comfort of your own space
